Places to buy/eat cheesecake!

Here you will find places to satisfy your cheesecake cravings

Places in London for cheesecake!

Ottolenghi

Offering a wide range of cakes and desserts, Ottolenghi have individual-sized cheesecakes with various toppings such as salted caramel!

Honey and Co

Famous for their cheesecake, Honey and Co offer an unbaked cheesecake on a katafiyeh base!
